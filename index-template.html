Required Configuration Files

The following CSV files are required for the simulation:

  • suppliers.csv - Information about suppliers
  • components.csv - All available components
  • supplier_pricing.csv - Component prices for each supplier
  • bike_models.csv - Definitions of bicycle models
  • production_requirements.csv - Labor requirements per model
  • pricing_tiers.csv - Pricing for each model and quality level
  • storage.csv - Storage facilities information
  • markets.csv - Market information
  • market_preferences.csv - Market preferences for models
  • market_price_sensitivity.csv - Market price sensitivity
  • seasonal_factors.csv - Seasonal demand factors
  • financial.csv - Financial parameters
  • initial_inventory.csv - Initial inventory
  • initial_staff.csv - Initial staff

Parameter Guidelines

The simulation is fully parametric, meaning you can modify any aspect:

  • Add new bike models by adding rows to bike_models.csv
  • Add new components in components.csv
  • Add new suppliers in suppliers.csv
  • Modify market preferences in market_preferences.csv
  • Change seasonal factors in seasonal_factors.csv
  • Adjust financial parameters in financial.csv
